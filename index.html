🧠 Who is Iron Man?

Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark, genius inventor and billionaire industrialist, created the Iron Man suit to survive capture and became a superhero. Known for his charisma, cutting-edge tech, and being a founding member of the Avengers, Iron Man is a symbol of futuristic human potential.
